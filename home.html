About Me

Hello, and welcome to my website!

My name is Bram, and I'm a developer from Gouda, the Netherlands. I first came in touch with development during my IT-traineeship at Qquest. It was so much fun that I was hooked right away. Since then, I've only grown more passionate about it and have decided that this is what I want to pursue in my career.

My development training started out with Java, with which I've learned to develop basic software. Accordingly, I started working with JavaScript along with related stack (HTML, CSS, NodeJs and React), to develop different kinds of web applications. Other stack I've worked with include Git, Linux, MySQL and Salesforce.
